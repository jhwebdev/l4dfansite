Left 4 Dead 1 has a less variety of weapons than its sequel. However, the "shove-back" system is the melee weapon of its time; the "shove-back" ability has no cool down so one can spam it infinitely. Weapons are organized into tiers; tier 1 and tier 2. Tier 1 weapons are found in the start of every campaign and are inferior than to those of tier 2 weapons. Tier 2 weapons while being more superior is harder to find and are usually only found once. Pistols have infinite ammo while other weapons are not. Aside from the weapons are the "gadgets" or "throwables". These "throwables" can only be carried one at time but can be found multiple times throughout a campaign. Throughout a campaign "ammo piles" will be found in safe rooms or in the the "crescendo" parts of the games.
The common infected is one of the basic classes of infected, although not very strong individually; large numbers of them can overwhelm the survivors. When they are all grouped up together in large numbers they are called "The Horde".
