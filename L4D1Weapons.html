Left 4 Dead 1 has a less variety of weapons than its sequel. However, the "shove-back" system is the melee weapon of its time; the "shove-back" ability has no cool down so one can spam it infinitely. Weapons are organized into tiers; tier 1 and tier 2. Tier 1 weapons are found in the start of every campaign and are inferior than to those of tier 2 weapons. Tier 2 weapons while being more superior is harder to find and are usually only found once. Pistols have infinite ammo while other weapons are not. Aside from the weapons are the "gadgets" or "throwables". These "throwables" can only be carried one at time but can be found multiple times throughout a campaign. Throughout a campaign "ammo piles" will be found in safe rooms or in the the "crescendo" parts of the games. All guns have flashlights that can be turned on and off.
The M1911 is the starting weapon in the beginning of each campaign. Although very basic it has infinite ammo and quick to reload.

M1911

The SMG has fastest fire rate and can do a good amount of damage because of its fire rate. It can be very loud because of its rate of fire.

Submachine Gun

The M16 is the only assualt rifle in the game with a burst fire only mode. It has a good 30 rounds magazine capacity.

M16 Assault Rifle

The Hunting Rifle has the highest amount of damage of all the other guns and has a scope with a very slow fire rate and reload time. It also makes a loud noise and has a low magazine capacity.

Hunting Rifle

The Tac Auto Shothgun has a faster fire rate and increased magazine capacity than that of the Pump Shotgun. However, it has a slow reload time (but faster than the Pump Shotgun) and lower damage output than the Pump Shotgun.

Tactical Auto Shotgun

The Pump Shotgun has a high damge output at the cost of magazine capacity and reload time.

Pump Shotgun

The Molotov, when thrown erupts in fire and covers an area in fire and burning any zombies (and survivors) that walk through it. The Pipe Bomb, when thrown will attract zombies to its beeping sound and will group up to it and then it will explode after a couple of seconds. The Bile Bomb, when thrown contains Boomer bile which will attract a larger amount of zombies and can be thrown on any surface or infected and other zombies will be attracted (or attack infected). Be careful of all throwables as it can hurt other survivors.
